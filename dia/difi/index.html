Wiki Diff

Most of the 5 million English Wikipedia articles have translations in at least one other language. Between different languages, though, the same Wiki article might vary in format, completeness, or bias. This is a quick-and-dirty tool for seeing what content you're missing in your native language without needing to understand the 288 languages that Wikipedia supports.

Look up an English Wikipedia article and select a language to compare it to. In green are Wiki topics only mentioned in the English version of the article. In red are the topics only mentioned in the foreign version. In yellow are topics that are shared between the two languages.
